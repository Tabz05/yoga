Yoga Pose Analyser

The yoga pose analyser will use webcam to identify a yoga pose from the following 3 poses: Tree right, Tree left and Warrior 2.

Tree right and Tree left indicate Tree pose formed using right and left leg respectively.

The frame size is 450 x 450. It is recommended to adjust the angle of laptop so that the entire body is visible.

Optimal results are found when the distance between camera and person is about 185 centimeters.

For best results, it is recommended to stand in front of a white background.

To stop yoga pose analysis, press 'q'.
